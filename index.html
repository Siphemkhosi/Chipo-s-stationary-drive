About Chipo's Stationery Drive

Chipos Stationery Drive (CSD) is a Non-Profitable Organization started on the 21 of March 2021 by Chipo Mafungwa a former top achiever from Palmridge Secondary School .This organization helps connect learners who have used or used stationery or school uniforms that they would like to give away to kids who are unable to afford themselves the luxury of having all the necessary learning tools a kid should have in order to get a good enough basic education. Since the government does not alway reach each and every citizen when delivering services they depend on NPO such as CSD to bridge the gap between the state and the people . This project started with Chipo giving away the school stationery that she thought she would not use since she was going to university ,but soon after that she realized that there were a lot more kids in need of stationery and uniform .
